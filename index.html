Wellington Internet Infrastructure

Traffic Application

The internet brings convenience to Traffic and Transportation. The public transportation such as bus and train always have timetable at station or online on the website.

1.Bus stop timetable

It can tell people buses information by using internet. Sometiems they cannot follow the timetable for some reason. But people can also see buses' real location.

2. Parking lot sign

The camera of the parking lot can see if there is any available space, then it will show on the sign. It also can show the price for parking.

3. Parking Payment Machine

The payment can be paid by Snapper or bank cards. Also, the machine is connected to internet.

4. Traffic Light

Every traffic lights are connected to each other. And they can be adjusted on the internet.

Public Infrastructure

1.Free Wifi Zone

There are many free wifi area around the city which can be easily found.

2. Vending Machine

Many of them are upgrated to connect to internet to make card payment available.

3. ATM

The ATM serves millions of customers every year. ATM's access the internet that holds people's banking account information.

4. Snapper Top Up Machine

Snapper Top Up Machine is also connected to internet in order to make transaction to top up the card.
